Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before you file a report.

Is NeighborFix run by the city?
It depends on who deploys NeighborFix. A city or county can run it as part of public works; a neighborhood association or civic group can run it for their area. The same product supports either model.
What makes a good report?
A specific location, one primary category, and a clear description. Avoid personal names or unrelated disputes — focus on fixable infrastructure and shared spaces.
How fast will someone respond?
Timelines depend on who operates the backend. The app is designed to show status so expectations stay clear.
Can I report emergencies here?
No. For police, fire, or medical emergencies, call your local emergency number. This tool is for non-emergency neighborhood issues.
