Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before you file a report.

Is NeighborFix run by KCCA or my district?
It depends on who deploys NeighborFix. A city authority (for example KCCA in Kampala), a district local government, or a neighbourhood association can run it. The same product supports any of those models.
What makes a good report?
A specific location (street, trading centre, parish), one primary category, and a clear description. Avoid personal names or unrelated disputes — focus on fixable infrastructure and shared spaces.
How fast will someone respond?
Timelines depend on who operates the service in your area. The app is designed to show status so expectations stay clear.
Can I report emergencies here?
No. For police, fire, or medical emergencies in Uganda, call 112 (national emergency line) or follow local instructions from your authority. NeighborFix is for non-emergency neighbourhood issues only.
